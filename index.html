Who We Are

Connecting Tanzania's Harvest to the World

Zaonex Agritrade Company Limited is a Tanzanian-registered agricultural export company, incorporated under the Companies Act, Cap. 212 and licensed by BRELA.

We bridge the gap between Tanzania's vast agricultural wealth and international markets. From smallholder farms across the Kilimanjaro, Morogoro, and Dodoma regions to buyers in Asia, Europe, the Middle East, and beyond — we handle every step of the export journey with precision and care.

Our approach is rooted in fairness — working directly with farming cooperatives and ensuring that the value chain benefits those who grow the crops.

What We Do

End-to-End Export Services

From farm gate to foreign port — we manage the entire agricultural export process so our clients receive quality produce with zero hassle.

01
🌾
Sourcing & Procurement

We work directly with smallholder farmers, cooperatives, and large-scale farms across Tanzania's agricultural zones to procure the finest crops at competitive prices.

  • Direct farmer partnerships across 10+ regions
  • Cooperative and farmer group sourcing
  • Competitive pricing with transparent reporting
  • Seasonal availability planning
02
⚙️
Processing & Grading

Before export, all produce goes through our quality control pipeline: cleaning, drying, sorting, grading, and packaging to meet international buyer specifications.

  • Machine-grade cleaning and sorting
  • Moisture content management
  • Custom size and quality grading
  • Food-grade packaging options
03
📋
Certification & Documentation

We handle all export documentation and certification requirements, including government-issued permits and internationally recognized quality certificates.

  • Phytosanitary certificates (TPHPA)
  • Certificate of Origin (TanTrade)
  • Organic & Fair Trade certifications
  • Commercial invoices & packing lists
04
🚢
Freight & Logistics

We coordinate sea freight from the Port of Dar es Salaam and Zanzibar, offering FCL (full container load) and LCL (less than container load) shipments to global ports.

  • Port of Dar es Salaam shipments
  • FCL and LCL container options
  • Refrigerated containers for perishables
  • Real-time shipment tracking
05
🛃
Customs Clearance

Our experienced customs team navigates Tanzanian export regulations, TRA requirements, and destination country import compliance to ensure smooth border crossings.

  • Tanzania customs (TRA) compliance
  • Export levy management
  • Destination import advisory
  • TANCIS system processing
06
🤝
Trade Advisory

We support international buyers and new-to-market importers with trade advisory services including market entry guidance, contract review, and quality assurance consultation.

  • Market entry & buyer matching
  • Contract & pricing consultation
  • Quality assurance advisory
  • Regulatory & compliance guidance
